Girls Who Game

The mission of Girls Who Game is to encourage a just and safe space-
at work, at home, and at heart. GWG encourages women and minorities to
work in the gaming industry in order to change the typical numbers in
the field. We want to change the overall portrayal of women in games
and the treatment of the women who play those games. Together, we can raise
awareness of these things and take the steps towards change.
